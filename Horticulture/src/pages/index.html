Having worked in the sector for the past 20 years upholding numerous roles, we feel that experienced twined with formal training is the perfect formula to delivery a service that is transparent, responsive and professional.

It is our belief that this combination sets us apart from our competitors and remain true to our values and services offered. We specialise in what we're exceptional at. This is the implementation of traditional gardening methods and core values while being dynamic and future focused.

Plant of the Month

Ixora

This flowering shrub is an old-time favorite in South Florida. With little maintenance and year-round blossoms, it's a gardener's dream. The flowers are often a reddish orange, but new varieties come in

yellow, pink, and even white. Plant in Central and South Florida in full sun with acidic conditions, and then sit back and watch the flowers bloom.
